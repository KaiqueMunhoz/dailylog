- Despite: Apesar de;
- Guidance: Orientação;
- Hold Someone Accountable: To consider someone responsible for something; to blame something on someone;

- Rather than: Em vez de;

- Whether: Se/When it has two options;
- Willing: Disposto/Interessado;
